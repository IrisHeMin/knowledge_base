这一期我们换个玩法——方法论盲盒。
A new format this time — Methodology Mystery Box.

很多时候我们做不好，不是不会，而是没有一套现成的方法来指挥我们怎么想、怎么说、怎么干。
这次我们一起认识 24 个"前人留下的小工具"，让以后的思考、表达、复盘都更轻松。
Often we struggle not because we lack the skill, but because we lack a ready-made framework to guide how to think, speak and act. Today we'll meet 24 classic mental tools to make thinking, expressing and reflecting easier.

📖 玩法说明（点开/收起）· How to Play (click to toggle)
  1. 开场分队：男生一队、女生一队（沪锡混合，不分地点）
    Split into Team Boys and Team Girls (cities mixed)
  2. 主持人点 🎲 抽一个辩题 → 出现话题 + 两队立场（系统随机分配，避免争"我们想站哪边"）
    Host clicks 🎲 Draw Topic — system assigns stances randomly (no "we want this side" arguments)
  3. 给两队各抽一个 方法论作为论证框架
    Draw a methodology for each team as the argument framework
  4. ⏱️ 30 秒小组讨论 → 选一个 / 几个代表准备
    30s group huddle → pick representative(s)
  5. ⏱️ 60 秒陈述：男队先，女队后。必须按抽到的方法论结构讲（5 个表达类方法：金字塔/SCQA/PREP/STAR/黄金圈，每个抽到时都会自动展开"一句话+模板+例子"）
    60s speech — Boys first, Girls second. Must follow the drawn method's structure (5 expression methods: Pyramid / SCQA / PREP / STAR / Golden Circle — each auto-expands hint + template + example)
  6. ⏱️ 30 秒自由辩（可选）
    30s free debate (optional)
  7. 主持人按 👨 / 👩 / 🤝 给胜方加分，进入下一轮
    Host scores 👨 / 👩 / 🤝 and moves to next round

🔥 最炸的点：方法论和话题会"乱配"出名场面，比如让你用 STAR 论证 番茄炒蛋先放糖还是先放盐——把"我外婆做了 40 年"讲出来谁能不笑？😎
🔥 The fun part: methods × topics get hilariously mismatched, e.g. using STAR to argue sugar-first vs salt-first scrambled eggs — "my grandma did it for 40 years" is gold 😎

🤖 救场机制：抽完方法后会出现 "AI 救场" 按钮，AI 会根据当前辩题 + 立场 + 方法直接给一段示范稿，照着讲 30 秒就够。
🤖 AI Rescue: After drawing a method, an "AI Rescue" button appears. It generates a sample speech tailored to the current topic + stance — just read it for 30s if you blank out.
